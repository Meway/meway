ABOUT THE COMPANY

The difference and our company

Quality

We offer a quality standard for cultured pearls. This means that we use a measuring system and price our pearls based off the size, shape, color, luster, and amount of imperfections in the product itself

Passion

We take pride in our work and our product. We work hard to deliver only the best quality and service to our customers, making sure that we won't disappoint you when you decide to buy our product

Design

Everything we do is to provide opportunity to our customers so we may on occasion switch partners. This means designers may vary. Be on the lookout for this to change because we aim for full customization

Support

Our support team is here to help. If you need anything at all, when it comes to our product, feel free to ask. We strive to get you the answers you need. You can find our contact information in the dropdown menu
